I am a first-year PhD student in the Department of Physics and Technology at the University of Tromsø. Prior to Tromsø, I was a research assistant professor in the Department of ECE and CSL at the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign (UIUC). During my entire career, I have been focusing on 2D material.
