I am a first-year PhD student in the Department of Physics and Technology at the University of Tromsø. I have been focusing on 2D materials, van der waals heterostructure and its applications.

Besides, I am interested in writing codes, especially Python, to solve and simulate questions. Recent side projects include simulation, soil classification, and power data visualization.
